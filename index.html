About Me

Hello! I'm Caitlyn, a fourth-year Psychology major at Mount Saint Mary's University in Los Angeles, CA. As a social scientist in training, I study people and the ways that culture effects our collective wellbeing. Using quantitative and experimental psychology, my research addresses global issues like the gender pay gap and the underrepresentation of women in leadership positions. As the President of the Honors Council and Ambassador at the Center for the Advancement of Women, I write articles and organize innovative programs that help diversify public service.

After graduation, I plan to pursue a Ph.D. in Social Psychology and help policy makers, governments, and public institutions solve problems using behavioral science.
